## What we do

Founded in 2017, Ruokaboksi/Yummy delivers recipes and ingredients straight to
customers' doors for a week of meals. The idea came from a simple
realization: the existing food system could be improved to reduce waste and
give people fresh, healthy, varied meals. And technology is what makes that
possible at scale.

> "Our target audience is every household that wants to eat fresh and varied
> food." — Juhana Rintala, CEO

Every week, customers pick 3–5 recipes and we deliver exactly the
ingredients they need, portioned and ready to cook. Today we serve customers
across Finland and, under the **Yummy** brand, in Czechia, Slovakia and Estonia.

## Our buffet

Our culture is characterized by ambition and a love for nutritious food. We
foster a collaborative environment with minimal hierarchy, where the tech
team's daily work is diverse and involves close collaboration across teams.

We operate hybrid and love meeting up to collaborate — join us at the office
in Helsinki or Turku for sprint change days and occasional events.

## Engineering

A TypeScript/Node.js monorepo running on **Google Cloud Platform**, built
around focused microservices communicating over HTTP and Pub/Sub, with
Prisma/PostgreSQL for persistence and Sanity CMS powering content and the
product catalog.

- **Front-end:** TypeScript, Vue (Nuxt), React (Next.js)
- **Back-end:** TypeScript, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL (Prisma)
- **Testing:** Vitest, Playwright
- **Infrastructure:** GCP, Cloud Run, Terraform, GitHub Actions
- **Data:** BigQuery, Data Studio, SQL
- **Integrations:** Stripe, Custobar, Sanity

We run a monorepo with Turborepo + pnpm and Biome for linting/formatting. We
favor shared libraries and consistent patterns across services over
one-off solutions.
